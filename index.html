Cruise Bar Roulette

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026

Plain-language summary: Cruise Bar Roulette does not require an account, display advertising, track your location, use analytics, or collect and upload your personal information.

1. About this policy

Cruise Bar Roulette is a free entertainment app provided by The Drunken Dolphin: Canada Division. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

2. Information we collect

Cruise Bar Roulette does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal information.

The app does not require or provide:

  • User accounts or sign-in
  • Advertising
  • Analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Location tracking
  • Contact-list access
  • Payment processing
  • Cloud storage of personal information

3. Information stored on your device

Certain app preferences and content may be stored locally on your device. This may include favorite drinks, drink ratings, ingredient preferences, filtering choices, and other app settings.

This locally stored information is used only to provide app features and personalize your experience. It is not uploaded to us or shared with third parties.

4. Photos and device permissions

Cruise Bar Roulette may allow you to add a photo to a drink. The app accesses the camera or photo library only when you choose to use that feature and grant the requested permission.

Photos selected or created through the app remain stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers or shared by us.

You can change camera or photo-library permissions at any time through your device settings.

5. Deleting locally stored information

You can remove locally stored information by clearing the app's storage through Android settings or by uninstalling the app. Individual favorites, ratings, photos, or preferences may also be removed through the app when that option is available.

6. Third-party services

Cruise Bar Roulette does not include third-party advertising or analytics services.

The app is distributed through Google Play. Google may process certain technical, diagnostic, purchase-account, or device information as part of providing the Google Play service. That processing is governed by Google's own policies and is separate from Cruise Bar Roulette.

7. Children's privacy

Cruise Bar Roulette does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users. The app is intended as a general cocktail-entertainment tool and is not specifically directed toward children.

8. Security

Because the app does not operate user accounts or transmit personal information to us, personal data is not stored on our servers. Information saved locally remains subject to the security protections of your device.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app's features or data practices change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when changes are made.
